## Core intention

Make Bevero sellable **tomorrow** with a small, honest pilot offer — without waiting for the full product vision. Preserve credibility by never promising more than the MVP pilot scope (Warenannahme, Lagerorte/Warenbewegungen, Auffüllliste Bar/Küche, Schichtübergabe, Freigabe/Korrektur, Audit Trail).

## Logic followed

- Sell operative relief, not AI: Zettelwirtschaft, WhatsApp-Chaos, verlorene Übergaben, Suchzeiten, fehlende Belegbarkeit.
- Bevero is positioned as the process layer *between* existing systems (POS, Warenwirtschaft, Buchhaltung stay untouched) — this is the strategic wedge and the objection-defuser.
- The pilot is deliberately tiny: one site, one or two processes, 4–6 weeks, honest abort criteria. A cleanly ended pilot counts as an acceptable outcome.
- Baum-OS / CoreOS / Company Brain stay out of all customer-facing language.

## Design assumptions

- Sender of outreach mails is the founder/operator personally, not a sales team.
- No final price list exists; all pricing is a suggested range marked `> Annahme:`.
- EU hosting + DSGVO-compliant Auftragsverarbeitung are planned but not yet documented — data answers stay deliberately vague until evidence exists.

## Tradeoffs

- Accepted:
  - No ROI numbers anywhere — less persuasive on paper, but survives skeptical Gastro operators.
  - Pilot scope is narrow enough that some prospects will find it "too small" — accepted to protect deliverability of promises.
- Rejected:
  - Selling the platform/vision story (rejected: creates expectations the MVP cannot meet).
  - Aggressive follow-up cadence (rejected: max two follow-ups after silence, then stop).

## Durable memory

- The one-sentence positioning: "Bevero macht Warenfluss, Auffülllisten und Schichtübergaben nachvollziehbar — als Ergänzung zu Kasse und Warenwirtschaft, nicht als deren Ersatz."
- No-fit signals are part of the kit (Betrieb zu klein, ERP-Erwartung, zentrale IT-Entscheidung, kein benannter Schmerz) — disqualifying early is a feature.

## Do not reuse blindly

- Email variant C (personal operative experience) requires the biographical claim to be true.
- Pricing ranges and DSGVO statements must be re-verified before every real offer until finalized.
- The pilot scope list mirrors the MVP as of 2026-07 — re-check against actual product state before reusing.

## Relation to Rauschenberger OS / Bevero

- location logic: Pilot is single-location by design; Standortlogik (Ausgabestellen, Lagerorte) is the audit's structural backbone.
- role/approval logic: Freigabe-/Korrekturprozess is sold as a first-class pilot component (who may approve corrections, visible trail).
- inventory/procurement/shift-planning logic: Procurement/Bestellwesen explicitly out of scope; shift handover (Übergabe) is in scope, shift *planning* is not.
- external-system boundary: POS, Warenwirtschaft, Buchhaltung are explicitly untouched — no integrations promised in the pilot.
